#### 1. 用户表(users)

**核心字段：**

- 基础字段：user_id, student_id(=读者证号), username, password, real_name, gender, birth_date, phone, email, avatar_url
- 读者专属：reader_type, department, major, enrollment_date, card_expire_date, account_status
- 借阅字段：current_borrow_count, max_borrow_count, fine_amount, last_borrow_time
- 系统审计：create_time, update_time, last_login_time, login_count, is_deleted
- 拓展字段：wechat_openid, remark

#### 2. 角色表(roles)

**核心字段：**

- role_id, role_name, role_code, description, parent_role_id, role_level, is_system_role, status, sort_order int DEFAULT 0 COMMENT '排序值，越小越靠前', create_time, update_time, creator_id, remark

#### 3. 权限表(permissions)

**核心字段：**

- permission_id, permission_name, permission_code, permission_type(menu/button/api), parent_permission_id, url_path, http_method, icon, sort_order, status, create_time, update_time, remark

#### 4. 用户角色关联表(user_roles)

**核心字段：**

- user_role_id, user_id, role_id, assign_time, assigner_id, expire_date, status, create_time, update_time, remark

#### 5. 角色权限关联表(role_permissions)

**核心字段：**

- role_permission_id, role_id, permission_id, assign_time, assigner_id, status, create_time, update_time, remark

#### 6. 图书表(books)

**核心字段：**

- 基础字段：book_id, book_name, author, isbn, publisher, publish_date, page_count, price, description, cover_url, category_id, tags
- 馆藏字段：shelf_location, rfid_tag, barcode, call_number, entry_date, source, donor
- 流通状态：collection_status, is_borrowable, current_borrower_id, due_date, total_borrow_count, last_borrow_time, last_return_time
- 审计字段：create_time, update_time, creator_id, updater_id, is_deleted

#### 7. 借阅记录表(borrow_records)

**核心字段：**

- 基础字段：borrow_record_id, borrow_no, user_id, book_id, borrow_time, due_date, return_time
- 业务字段：borrow_type, renew_count, borrow_status, operator_id, operation_time, update_time, is_deleted, remark, is_overdue
- RFID模拟字段：rfid_device_id, rfid_device_ip, rfid_read_status, rfid_read_time

#### 8. RFID绑定历史表(rfid_bind_history)

**核心字段：**

- id, book_id, rfid_tag, bind_time, unbind_time, operator_id, operation_type, remark

#### 9. 审计日志表(audit_logs)

**核心字段：**

- audit_log_id, operation_type, operation_desc, create_time, result_code, user_id, username, log_level, ip_address, user_agent, request_method, request_url, request_params, response_data, execution_time, error_message, module_name, remark

#### 10. 异常记录表(exceptions)

**核心字段：**

- exception_id, exception_type, exception_level(info/warn/error), create_time, exception_source, exception_message, exception_details, user_id, username, ip_address, user_agent, request_url, request_method, request_summary, stack_trace_summary, resolved_status, resolver_id, resolve_time, resolve_note

#### 11. 图书分类表(book_categories)

**核心字段：**

- category_id, category_name, category_code, parent_category_id, category_level, sort_order, description, status, create_time, update_time, creator_id, remark

#### 12. 图书预约表(book_reservations)

**核心字段：**

- reservation_id, user_id, book_id, reservation_time, expire_time, pickup_deadline, pickup_time, cancel_time, reservation_status(pending/available/picked_up/cancelled/expired), notification_sent, operator_id, create_time, update_time, remark

#### 13. 罚款记录表(fine_records)

**核心字段：**

- fine_id, user_id, borrow_record_id, fine_type(overdue/damage/lost), fine_amount, paid_amount, fine_status(unpaid/paid/waived), fine_time, pay_time, overdue_days, operator_id, waive_reason, create_time, update_time, remark

#### 14. 图书状态变更历史表(book_status_history)

**核心字段：**

- history_id, book_id, old_status, new_status, change_reason, operator_id, rfid_device_id, rfid_device_ip, change_time, create_time, remark
